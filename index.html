#PROJECTNOON

January 20th, 2017, 12:00 pm:
Donald Trump becomes President of the United States.

At noon, stand with us, and say: we are not a divided country. We are all Americans.

How to Participate

1

COMMIT

Promise to donate to an organization that protects people whose rights were threatened by Trump’s campaign.

2

MOBILIZE

Get others to do the same.

3

ACT

Make your donation—and a statement—at noon on January 20th.

4

UNITE

Share your donation on social media with #projectnoon.

Don’t know where to start? These people do:

Immigration

The Capital Area Immigrants’ Rights Coalition

Provides wide-ranging services to help defend immigrants in the immigration and criminal justice arenas.

Poverty

The Legal Services Corporation

Provides funding to local legal aid organizations that provide free or discounted legal services to those who cannot afford legal assistance.

Civil Rights

The American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee

Provides wide-ranging services to protect and defend the rights of people of Arab descent.

LGBTQ Rights

The Trevor Project

Provides crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and questioning young people ages 13-24.

Environment

The Environmental Defense Fund

Committed to bipartisan environmental efforts that benefit people while protecting natural systems.
